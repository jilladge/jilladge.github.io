  1. Home

Landmark information

  • Built: 12th century
  • Suggested Duration: 3 hours
  • Fun Fact: Has a hidden tower that was re-discovered during excavations that took place in 1912, in the back of a canteen coal cellar window. David's Tower was built in the 1380s by King David as part of a reconstruction program of the castle. It stood 100 feet high.

A Fortress of Legends

Immerse yourself in the rich tapestry of Scotland's past within the mighty walls of Edinburgh Castle. Discover the tales of kings and queens, warriors and poets, and the pivotal events that have shaped a nation.

Dominating the Skyline: Perched atop an extinct volcano, Edinburgh Castle not only captivates with its history but also offers breathtaking panoramic views of the city below.
