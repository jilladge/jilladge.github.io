  1. Home

Landmark information

  • Built: 12th century, present buildings mostly built between 1490 and 1600
  • Suggested Duration: 1 hour
  • Fun Fact: Home of Mary Queen of Scots

A Castle Beyond Compare

Perched majestically atop Castle Hill, Stirling Castle is an architectural masterpiece and an emblem of Scotland's past. It has witnessed epic battles, royal intrigues, and tales of valor for centuries.

From the castle's battlements, behold breathtaking views of the Scottish countryside. Survey the rolling hills, meandering rivers, and lush landscapes that have inspired poets and artists for generations.

Explore the sumptuous royal apartments and walk in the footsteps of Mary, Queen of Scots, and understand the significance of this historic stronghold.
